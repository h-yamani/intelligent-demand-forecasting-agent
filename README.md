## Overview
Production-oriented demand forecasting system with strong focus on time-series understanding and feature engineering.

---

## Problem
Multi-series forecasting across stores and items with strong seasonality, noise, and external effects.

---

## Approach
- Time series analysis (trend, seasonality, autocorrelation)
- Advanced feature engineering (lag, rolling, cyclical)
- Structured reporting and validation

---

## Key Insights
- Temporal dependency dominates demand
- Weekly seasonality is strong
- Promotions significantly impact sales
- Price has weak direct effect
