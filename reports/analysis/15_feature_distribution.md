# Feature Analysis: Sales Distribution

## Objective
To analyze the statistical distribution of the target variable.

## Visualization
Histogram with density curve.

## Key Observations
- Right-skewed distribution
- Presence of extreme high values
- Non-normal shape

## Interpretation
Demand includes occasional high spikes, likely due to promotions or external events.

## Feature Justification
- Tree-based models are appropriate for skewed data
- Suggests robustness to outliers is required
- May consider log transformation if needed

## Conclusion
Sales distribution is non-linear and skewed, requiring flexible modeling approaches.
