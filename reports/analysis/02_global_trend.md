# Global Daily Sales Trend Analysis

## What does this plot show?
This plot shows total daily sales aggregated across all stores and items over time.

## Key Observations

### 1. Strong yearly seasonality
Sales follow a repeating yearly pattern with peaks and troughs.

### 2. Positive growth trend
Sales peaks increase over time, indicating overall demand growth.

### 3. Weekly seasonality
High-frequency oscillations indicate weekly patterns (weekday effect).

### 4. Peak periods
Highest sales occur mid-year.

### 5. Low periods
Lowest sales occur at the beginning of each year.

## What can we understand?

- Demand has both **trend and seasonality**
- Any forecasting model must capture:
  - yearly seasonality
  - weekly seasonality
- External factors (promo, holidays) likely affect peaks

## Business implications

- Prepare inventory before peak seasons
- Allocate more resources during high-demand periods
- Plan promotions strategically

## Conclusion

The data exhibits strong seasonality and an increasing trend, making time-series forecasting essential for accurate demand prediction.
