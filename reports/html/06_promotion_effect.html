Key Insights

  • Consistent effect: stable across observations

Business Interpretation

Promotions are a key driver of demand and can be strategically used to boost revenue and manage inventory.

Modeling Implications

  • Include promo as a core feature
  • Combine with price and time features
  • Critical for accurate forecasting

Conclusion

Promotional events significantly increase demand. Proper modeling of this effect is essential for any forecasting system.
